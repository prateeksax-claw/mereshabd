काव्य-जगत

World of Magic

If only I had a magic wand,

It would soothe every pain.

I'd live this day every single day,

With dreams, some new, some incomplete,

Urging me to take flight once again.

It would free all from sorrow ,

Filling each moment with joy.

Paths would be easy and radiant,

Success would scatter in courtyard.

If only I could find that wand,

It would gather scattered bonds,

Teach the meaning of closeness,

Bridge distances, bring hearts nearer,

And narrate stories in vivid colors.

At "Open Sesame," doors would yield,

Every wish granted with no knock needed.

I'd hold that wand like a fairy queen,

Spin magic before children's gleaming eyes.

If only laughter sparkled in innocent faces,

A city of dreams would come alive.

Wings would freely embrace the skies,

And incomplete canvases would thrive.

To bridge is our duty …

Necessary in healing this magical world,

May today fulfill all aspirations,

And bring success, endlessly.

Jan7

Dedicated to Granddaughter on her Birthday

on 11th Jan 2025

ILA कविता

Jyoti - A Wonderful Experience

' JYOTI' … A WONDERFUL EXPERIENCE

It was always had a pleasure, and holding hands

Never forget experience as Walk along, its journey,

And feel pride, And a sense happiness, To reach its deadline.

With task assigned as mission. To achieve and fulfil the goal.

A Path well nurtured and as our nature. All women for women

Who always aim for future, For keeping ILA flag on its Flight.

Always reigns in creativity and took Jyoti always to new height.

Every year, its nature to celebrate women hood as "Slogan',

A new platform to fly, And forge new destiny as dear.

An opportunity to excel as women with perspective and vision,

Its physical strength gives Liberty and freedom to think and do,

Reach out talents deep within, Work with them was pleasure.

To achieve the target, is its MOTO,

Our journey through all years on…… [1977to 2017]

Faced many up and down, challenges on its path, 'BUT'

Always walked with no fear to cross, And obstacles won. .

'JYOTI' never looked behind, keeps going with strange path,

कवनी - जन्मदिन

नन्ही कवू के जन्मदिन पर

काश! हम कैद कर पाते, उस अहसास को, भावपूर्ण के सब क्षण,

ले पाते अनगिनत अवसर, दे पाते आलिंगन, प्यार भरी झप्पी-पप्पी।

खुश है दादा दादी, है जन्मदिन उसका, देख बिटिया को फलते-फूलते,

ईश्वर का दिया उपहार, है परिवार खजाने का, अनमोल रतन हमारा,

आज छह वर्ष की है हुई, हमारी प्यारी सी 'कवू' मुस्कान बिखराती परी।

माता पिता को ढेर सी बधाइयाँ व शुभकामनाएं।

दादा-दादी 11 जनवरी 2023

उपजिंदर के नाम

कदर-ए-कीमत

यह जोर आजमाइश की बात,

यह आखिरी लडाई की बात,

ये बमों के धमाके ,

ये मिसाइलों के पटाखे,

इस हथियारों की होड़ में,

जरूरत है लोगों को मरहम की,

कोई अब क्यों? याद नहीं करता,

हिरोशिमा की हालत-ए-जंग को।

भूल गये हैं, हम अब सब

इन्सानियत की अहमियत को,

मानो अब कम हो गई है,

याददाश्त इनसानों की।

ऐसा लगता है, कि बढ़ रहा है,

मानव सिर्फ पतन की ओर,

अब क्यों? कोई नहीं है करता,

प्यार और मोहब्बत की बातें।

इस मदहोश दुनिया में इन्सानों की

ना ही कोई कीमत, न ही कोई कदर।

— उपजिंदर
