Health is Wealth, So Invest Wisely

गीतांजलि सक्सेना
जुलाई २०२५
होम › रचनाएं › Health is Wealth, So Invest Wisely
In essence

A thoughtful reflection on the wisdom of living well. Drawing on lived experience and observation, this essay explores why health — of body, mind and spirit — is the only wealth that truly matters.

'Health is wealth, so invest wisely'

'नेक तंदुरुस्ती लाख नियामत'

In our fast-paced modern life, health often takes a back seat until we are confronted with illness or face unexpected health issues. Yet, the truth is simple and powerful… the choices we make today invests in shaping the quality of our health for tomorrow. Investing in health is not just for avoiding diseases, it is building a strong foundation for a long, happy, and more fulfilling life.

It means in health… dedicated time, effort and to practices that strengthen our physical, mental and emotional well-being. Unlike financial investments, the returns of health investments are often invisible in the short term but immensely valuable in the long run.

'Key areas in health investment'

  • Balanced nutrition: Eating nutrients rich foods for body fuels.
  • Regular physical activity: Keeps our heart, muscles, and bone strong while optimize weight and improves mood.
  • Preventive care: Routine checkup, vaccination, and health screening catch risky issues early, when they are still easy to treat.
  • Mental health care: Stress management, mindfulness and seeking help when needed, supports emotional resilience.

'The Benefits of early Health Investment'

  • Lower risk of chronic illnesses like diabetes and heart disease.
  • Stronger immune system to fight infections.
  • Enhanced energy levels and productivity.
  • Better mental clarity and emotional balance.
  • Assures longer life expectancy with a high quality of life.

Think this way. Small daily efforts like walking, a home cooked meal, and at least ten minutes of meditation are long term deposits in health saving accounts that would pay off in the future.

'We can't Clap with One Hand (ताली एक हाथ से नहीं बजती)'

The role of Community and Government also plays a big role in supporting health investments. Affordable Health insurance Policies promote clean water and air pollution free schemes. Above all, health educational programs empower everyone, specifically women and children who are under privileged in resources.

Towards a Healthier Tomorrow… With Expert guidance

Welcome to this first Chapter, in new column @Mereshabd.com. I believe that the path to a healthier future begins with knowledge and expert guidance. This special column space is dedicated to empower you all with insight from leading Medical Specialist Experts from all across the medical field.

Health is our most precious asset. By investing in our health today, we give ourselves and our loved ones a precious gift… a healthier, and stronger tomorrow. Let's begin this journey together… because your health deserves attention now, today… to build a better tomorrow.

— Gitanjali Saxena 2025
